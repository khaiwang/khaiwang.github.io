I’m a Master’s student and obtained my B.E in Renmin University of China (RUC) under the supervision of Prof. Yunpeng Chai in 2021. I’m currently also a research intern student in the Data Management Group in TU Darmstadt, advised by Prof. Carsten Binnig and Dr. Tobias Ziegler. My research interests lie in the development of components for modern data-intensive systems, especially disaggregated systems. At present, my focus is on constructing a scalable transactional database system above the high-speed network-supported shared-caching distributed system ScaleStore. I am on track to graduate in June 2024 and am actively seeking opportunities for PhD positions.
